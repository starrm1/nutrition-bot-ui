## Understanding common dietary patterns and their health outcomes

### Description
A detailed exploration of prevalent dietary patterns, their characteristics, and the associated health outcomes. The goal is to enable better dietary choices based on scientific data.

### Scoring: 95%

### Key Learnings
- Mastered recognition and classification of major dietary patterns: Mediterranean, DASH, plant-based, ketogenic, paleo, and intermittent fasting.
- Identified biomarkers and health outcomes associated with each dietary pattern.
- Developed algorithms for pattern matching from patient food logs.

### Research Findings
- Mediterranean and DASH diets consistently associated with reduced cardiovascular disease risk.
- Plant-based dietary patterns linked to lower BMI and improved insulin sensitivity.
- Ketogenic patterns effective for short-term weight loss and glycemic control in type 2 diabetes.
