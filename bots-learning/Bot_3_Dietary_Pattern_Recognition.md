### Research Findings
- Mediterranean diet: 25% reduction in cardiovascular disease risk (PREDIMED study, NEJM)
- DASH diet: reduces systolic blood pressure by 8–14 mmHg in hypertensive individuals (NIH/NHLBI)
- Plant-based diets: reduce type 2 diabetes risk by ~23% (PLOS Medicine, 2019)

### Current Focus Areas
